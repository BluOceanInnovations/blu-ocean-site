What Is AEO?

AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization — the practice of optimizing your business to be surfaced and recommended by AI-powered tools, not just traditional search engines.

When someone asks ChatGPT "Who's the best divorce attorney in Phoenix?" or asks Perplexity "What's a highly-rated medical spa near me?" — those are the moments AEO wins or loses.

SEO gets you ranked in blue links. AEO gets you recommended by AI. Both matter. But AEO is where new clients are increasingly being won.

SEO

Targets keyword rankings in Google's blue link results

AEO

Targets AI summaries, recommendation boxes, and assistant responses

SEO

Measured in clicks and impressions

AEO

Measured in citations, recommendations, and shortlist mentions

SEO

Built on keywords and backlinks

AEO

Built on E-E-A-T signals, structured data, and verifiable credibility

Frequently Asked Questions

AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews evaluate businesses based on E-E-A-T signals: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trust. This includes your Google Business Profile completeness, review volume and recency, named team members, structured website content, and consistent information across the web. The businesses AI recommends are the ones it can verify with confidence.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your business to be surfaced and recommended by AI-powered tools — not just traditional search engines. SEO targets keyword rankings in Google's blue links. AEO targets the AI summary box, the recommendation in ChatGPT, and the "best option" result in Perplexity. Both matter, but AEO is the frontier where new clients are increasingly being won.

Most clients see measurable improvements in AI visibility within 60–90 days of implementing our Solidify + Amplify + Elevate framework. The timeline depends on your starting point — businesses with an existing Google Business Profile and some reviews move faster than those starting from scratch. Our SAE-AI framework (Solidify → Amplify → Elevate) ensures you build trust before spending on promotion, so every marketing dollar works harder.
